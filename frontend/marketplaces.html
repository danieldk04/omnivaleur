How it works

From one item to all of Europe

Omnivaleur makes cross-listing so easy you'll never have to think about it again.

1

Install the Chrome extension

The extension runs in the background and automatically detects your products on the platforms you already use. Install once, always active.

2

Import your existing items

Open an existing listing on Marktplaats or Vinted — Omnivaleur automatically imports photos, description, price and category to your dashboard.

3

Choose your target markets

Select which marketplaces and countries to list in. Choose from 10+ platforms across 27 EU countries.

4

Omnivaleur publishes everything automatically

Our extension fills in forms, adapts categories per platform and posts your item. You don't have to do a thing — we do the work.

5

Sell · sync · repeat

Once an item sells on one platform, Omnivaleur automatically removes it everywhere else. No more double sales or outdated listings.
