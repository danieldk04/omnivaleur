Help & guide
How does CrossList EU work?

🧩 Chrome extension required

CrossList EU publishes items via a Chrome extension. The extension automatically logs in to Marktplaats, 2dehands and Vinted and posts your listing.

Without the extension, publishing does not work.

Download Chrome extension →

📋 How does it work?

1. Create an item
Click "New item", fill in the details and upload photos.
2. Choose platforms
Select which marketplaces to publish to and click "Save & publish".
3. Extension does the rest
The Chrome extension opens the platforms in the background and posts your listing automatically.
4. Sold?
Mark an item as sold and all other listings are automatically removed.

Automatic protections
CrossList EU resolves these issues automatically — you don't have to do anything.
🔄
Marktplaats auto-relist
✓ Active — every 6 hours
Free Marktplaats listings expire silently after 28 days. CrossList checks every 6 hours and automatically relists after 27 days so you stay visible.
Without this: listing disappears after 28 days without warning.
🛡️
Vinted photo protection
✓ Active — every upload
Vinted detects identical photos from other platforms via pixel-hashing and may ban your account. CrossList slightly modifies each photo via canvas rendering — invisible to the eye, but a unique hash for Vinted.
Without this: risk of account ban on Vinted.
🧹
2dehands formatting fix
✓ Active — every publish
2dehands only supports 7 HTML tags in descriptions. Other tags crash the editor. CrossList automatically strips all unsupported formatting before publishing.
Without this: publish fails or description is empty.
🌐
Auto-translation
✓ Active — Marktplaats & 2dehands
Marktplaats and 2dehands are Dutch-language platforms. You enter everything in English — CrossList automatically translates titles and descriptions to Dutch before publishing.
Without this: English text visible on Dutch-language platforms.
⚡
Sale sync
✓ Active — all platforms
Once an item sells on one platform, all other active listings are automatically removed. No double sales, no manual cleanup.
Without this: risk of double sales and negative reviews.
📋
Marktplaats editor fix
✓ Active — every publish
Marktplaats uses its own rich-text editor (Lexical) that does not respond to standard DOM input. CrossList fills the editor correctly via the internal EditorState API.
Without this: description stays empty on publish.
