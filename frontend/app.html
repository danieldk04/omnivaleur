Automatic protections

CrossList EU handles these issues automatically — you don't have to do anything.

🔄
Marktplaats auto-relist
✓ Active — every 6 hours
Free Marktplaats listings expire silently after 28 days. CrossList checks every 6 hours and automatically relists after 27 days so you stay visible.
Without this: your listing disappears after 28 days without any warning.
🛡️
Vinted photo protection
✓ Active — every upload
Vinted detects identical photos from other platforms via pixel-hashing. CrossList slightly modifies each photo via canvas — invisible to the eye, unique hash for Vinted.
Without this: risk of account ban on Vinted.
🧹
2dehands formatting fix
✓ Active — every publish
2dehands only supports 7 HTML tags in descriptions. CrossList automatically strips all unsupported formatting before publishing.
Without this: publish fails or description is empty.
🇳🇱
Dutch auto-translation
✓ Active — Marktplaats & 2dehands
You enter listings in English — CrossList automatically translates titles and descriptions to Dutch before publishing on Marktplaats and 2dehands.
Without this: English text visible on Dutch-language platforms.
⚡
Sale sync
✓ Active — all platforms
Once an item sells on one platform, all other active listings are automatically removed. No double sales, no manual cleanup.
Without this: risk of double sales and negative reviews.
📋
Marktplaats editor fix
✓ Active — every publish
Marktplaats uses a custom rich-text editor (Lexical) that ignores standard DOM input. CrossList fills it correctly via the internal EditorState API.
Without this: description stays empty on publish.

📋 How it works — step by step

1
Install the Chrome extension
Download the CrossList EU extension from the Chrome Web Store and log in with your account credentials. The extension runs silently in the background — you don't need to open it again after setup.
2
Create an item
Click + New item, fill in the title, description, price, size and upload your photos. Write everything in English — CrossList EU handles the Dutch translation for Marktplaats & 2dehands automatically.
3
Choose platforms and publish
Select which marketplaces to list on — Marktplaats, 2dehands, Vinted or Shopify — and click Save & publish. The publishing job is instantly added to the queue.
4
Extension publishes automatically
Within 15 seconds the Chrome extension picks up the job, opens the marketplace in a background tab, fills in all fields, uploads your photos and submits the listing. Fully automated — the tab closes when done.
5
Item sold? Mark it once
When your item sells on any platform, click Mark as sold in the dashboard. CrossList EU automatically removes the listing from all other platforms — no double sales.

🖥 Dashboard explained

Total items
All items you have added, regardless of status.
Active listings
Items live on at least one marketplace.
Not published
Created but not yet sent to any platform.
Queued
Jobs waiting for the extension to execute.
The yellow banner appears when jobs are queued. It disappears once the extension has processed them. If it stays >1 min, check your extension.

❓ Frequently asked questions

Why is my listing stuck in "Queued"?
The Chrome extension is either not installed, not logged in, or Chrome isn't running. Click the extension icon and make sure it shows "Extension active — ready to publish". Once active, it picks up the job within 15 seconds.
The extension opens a tab but the listing doesn't get submitted — what's wrong?
You are probably not logged in to that marketplace in Chrome. Open Marktplaats, 2dehands or Vinted manually, log in, then try publishing again. The extension uses your existing browser session.
Do I need to log in to the marketplaces separately?
Yes — once. Log in to Marktplaats, 2dehands and Vinted in Chrome at least once. Your session will be remembered. CrossList EU never stores your marketplace passwords.
Can I write my listings in English?
Yes — always write in English. CrossList EU automatically translates your title and description to Dutch for Marktplaats and 2dehands. Vinted and Shopify use the English text directly.
What happens when an item sells?
Click Mark as sold on the item. CrossList EU queues delist jobs for all other platforms and the extension removes those listings automatically — preventing double sales.
Which platforms are supported?
Currently: Marktplaats, 2dehands, Vinted and Shopify. eBay, Etsy, Kleinanzeigen and Facebook Marketplace are in development. Marktplaats, 2dehands and Vinted use the Chrome extension; Shopify connects via the official API.
Are my marketplace passwords stored?
No. CrossList EU never sees or stores your marketplace passwords. The extension uses the browser session you already have open. Only your CrossList EU account password is used to log in to this dashboard.
Can I use CrossList EU on multiple devices?
The dashboard works on any device and browser. The Chrome extension needs to be installed on the specific computer where publishing should happen. Jobs are processed by whichever Chrome has the extension active.
